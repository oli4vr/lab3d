How to start?
-------------
Type the following on the console or terminal:
 > lab3d


How to play?
------------
Use arrow keys left and right to rotate and forward to walk forward in the current direction.
Find the exit (marked by E).
Every level you get a more complex labyrinth.


Why did I create this?
----------------------
Just to learn and get familiar with the ncurses library and I based it on a program i wrote for my old TI-85 calculator when I was 16 years old. Oh the memories... ;)
